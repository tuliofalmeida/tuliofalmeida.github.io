# 4EU+ Entrepreneurial and intrapreneurial project management

The [4EU+ project](https://4euplus.eu/4EU-850.html) is a one-year international diploma programme that trains students in entrepreneurship and innovative project management. Through online courses and a real-world team project, the participants learn how to design and lead sustainable, socially impactful solutions. The diploma is offered by the universities that are part of the 4EU+ Allience: Sorbonne University, Charles University, Heidelberg University and the Universities of Geneva, Milan and Warsaw.

I’m part of the [HOLISTICA](https://www.linkedin.com/company/holistica-onehealth/?viewAsMember=true) team, and our project aims to advance the One Health approach by connecting human, animal, and environmental health systems through integrated analysis, research, and cross-sector collaboration.
